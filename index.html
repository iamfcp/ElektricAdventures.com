Our Amazing Services

SUP

Relax gliding over the water in a Stand Up Paddleboard. And ride into the sunset in the summer breeze. Rental from 25 per hour including floating vest.

ONEWHEEL

Prefer glidng on land? we got you covered. Surf over any hard surface with a powered board. From 30 per hour or 50 with instruction.

ONEWHEEL PARTY

Celebrate your special day with all your friends riding onewheels, even if they have never done it, go from zero to hero.

KITESURF

Want more adrenaline? try a kitesurfing lesson and master this new and upcoming sport. Introductory course from 350 or 200 per person in groups of up to 3 people.

WIND WING

Enjoy the newest wind sport, a mix of SUP, surf, kite, in sum all the fun.

E-BIKE

Enjoy all year riding the tracks with assisted power.

SAILING

Ride the waters accompanied of your friends in a sailboat. From 150 per person, groups of 4-6 people, 3 hours ride.
